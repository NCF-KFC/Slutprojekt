Ferrari

Ferrari is an iconic Italian luxury sports car manufacturer, founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939. Known for its high-performance vehicles and success in Formula 1 racing, Ferrari produces exclusive, expertly crafted cars that combine advanced technology with stunning design. Based in Maranello, Italy, the brand is famous for models like the Ferrari 488 and LaFerrari, embodying speed, innovation, and a rich motorsport heritage.

History and strenght

  • SF90:The Ferrari SF90 is a plug-in hybrid supercar that blends cutting-edge technology with Ferrari’s legendary craftsmanship. With a focus on sustainability and advanced design, it offers a refined driving experience in both coupe and convertible versions, embodying the brand’s commitment to luxury and innovation.
  • 12 Cilindri: The Ferrari 12 Cilindri lineup features vehicles powered by 12-cylinder engines, offering unmatched performance and luxury. Models like the 812 Superfast and GTC4Lusso combine power, precision, and refined design, delivering an iconic driving experience that reflects Ferrari's engineering excellence.
  • 812 competizione:The Ferrari 812 Competizione, introduced in 2021, is a track-focused variant of the 812 Superfast. Designed for exceptional performance, it features weight reduction, upgraded aerodynamics, and advanced suspension systems. With sharper handling and refined driving dynamics, it stands as one of Ferrari's most thrilling and exclusive models.
  • Daytona SP3:The Ferrari Daytona SP3, introduced in 2021, is a limited-edition sports car inspired by Ferrari's 1967 racing legacy. Part of the "Icona" series, it blends classic design with modern performance, featuring a carbon-fiber body and a driver-focused interior. With only 599 units produced, the Daytona SP3 is a rare and exclusive collectible.
